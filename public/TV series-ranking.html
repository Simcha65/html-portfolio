The Best TV Series According To Oscar

My top 3 TV series of all-time.

The Queen's Gambit

Though fictional, it was a sit by the edge of your seat mini-series
covering the life of a prodigy female from childhood to adulthood and
the obstacles she faces.

Star Trek Generations

This series reveals the potential humans have if we all work
together. The ends of the universe are ours.

Star Trek Voyager

Another Star Trek series revealing the potential humans have in facing
unimaginable constraints, but then overcoming and being victorious by shear
human determination.
